FAQ

When does the club meet?
Thursdays, although this sometimes varies based on member availability

What does the club offer?
A supportive environment!! Aaaand the club has discounted $12 passes to Rock Spot. There are also harnesses and climbing shoes available for members without their own gear to share. Some more experienced climbers have offered to teach newer climbers -- unofficial classes will occur based on interest

How much are dues?
Dues are $25/year
