Welcome to the EBAC Rock Climbing Club

Club Info

The club meets about once a week -- for Summer 2019 they'll be meeting after work on Tuesdays or Thursdays. Based on interest and availability, some longer weekend trips may go out as well!
Members regularly meet up at Lantern Hill and nearby climbing gyms.
Everyone is here to support each other -- in the past more experienced climbers have taught newer climbers skills such as belaying and setting anchors.
Dues are $25/year
