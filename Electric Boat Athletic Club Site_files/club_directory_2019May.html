Club Info
  • The club meets about once a week -- for Summer 2019 we'll be meeting during the day on Wednesdays or Fridays (depending on weather!). Based on interest and availability, groups may go out on other weekdays or weekends. Stay tuned for farther trips to different rocks!
  • Members regularly meet up at Lantern Hill and nearby climbing gyms.
  • Some gear is available for all club members to use:
    • harness
    • climbing shoes
    • helmet
    • belay device
    • discounted $12 passes to Rock Spot
  • More experienced climbers teach newer climbers skills such as belaying and setting anchors.
  • Dues are $25/year
  • Contact: For more information email Gabe Schwartz, gschwart@gdeb.com
Climbing Destinations
    Lantern Hill is a small climbing wall a stone's throw from EB North Stonington
    In central CT Ragged Mountain has about 90 routes and has both Trad and Toprope routes available
    Two nearby gyms are Rock Spot in Peacedale, RI and Central Rock Gym in Warwick, RI or Glastonbury, CT.
