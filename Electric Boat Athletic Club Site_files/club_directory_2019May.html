Club Info

    The club meets about once a week -- for Summer 2019 we'll be meeting after work on Tuesdays or Thursdays. Based on interest and availability, groups may go out on other weekdays or weekends. Stay tuned for farther trips to Ragged Mt. or the Buggs!
    Members regularly meet up at Lantern Hill and nearby climbing gyms.
    Members are here support each other -- in the past more experienced climbers have taught newer climbers skills such as belaying and setting anchors.
    Dues are $25/year
